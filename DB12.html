Aston Martin DBX 707

The Aston Martin DB12 is a luxury sports car that represents the next chapter in the brand's iconic DB series. Launched in 2023, it combines cutting-edge technology with the brand's signature elegance. Powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine, it delivers 671 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, offering impressive acceleration and top-tier performance. The DB12 boasts a refined, hand-crafted interior with advanced infotainment features, ensuring both comfort and style for its occupants. With its aggressive yet graceful design, the DB12 remains true to Aston Martin's legacy while introducing modern enhancements that appeal to a new generation of automotive enthusiasts.
